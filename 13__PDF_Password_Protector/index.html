‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you store my PDF files or passwords?
No! All files and passwords are processed on our secure server and are immediately deleted after processing. We never store, log, or keep any of your files or passwords.
Q: How secure is the password protection?
We use industry-standard PDF encryption. The strength of your protection depends on your password - use a long, complex password for maximum security.
Q: What if my PDF is already password-protected?
You'll receive an error message. You need to remove the existing password first before adding a new one.
Q: Can I recover my password if I forget it?
No. We don't store your password, so there's no way to recover it. Make sure to remember or safely store your password before protecting the PDF.
Q: What makes a strong password?
Use at least 8 characters with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. Avoid common words or personal information.
