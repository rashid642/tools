mic About Audio Test

lightbulb What Does This Tool Do?

Audio Test is a comprehensive tool for testing your microphone and speakers. Record audio with real-time visualization, test stereo channels (left, right, or both), and verify your audio setup before important calls or recordings. Perfect for troubleshooting audio issues and ensuring optimal sound quality.

rocket_launch Key Features

  • Live Waveform Visualization: See your audio input in real-time with beautiful waveforms
  • Audio Recording: Record up to 1 minute of audio and play it back to test your microphone
  • Stereo Channel Control: Test left, right, or both speakers independently
  • Seekable Playback: Click or drag the progress bar to jump to any timestamp
  • Input Level Meter: Monitor your microphone input levels visually
  • Download Recordings: Save your test recordings for later analysis
  • Sample Rate Info: View audio sample rate and recording statistics

checklist How to Use

  • Step 1: Click "Start Recording" to activate your microphone (grant permission if prompted)
  • Step 2: Speak into your microphone and watch the waveform visualization
  • Step 3: Monitor the input level meter to ensure proper audio levels
  • Step 4: Click "Stop Recording" when finished (max 1 minute, auto-stops)
  • Step 5: Select channel (Left, Both, or Right) and play back your recording
  • Step 6: Download your recording if needed for further testing

help Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why can't I hear my recording?
Make sure your speakers or headphones are connected and volume is turned up. Check your system audio settings if the issue persists.
Q: What's the purpose of the channel selector?
The channel selector lets you test your left and right speakers independently to ensure your stereo setup is working correctly.
Q: How long can I record for?
Recordings are limited to 1 minute (60 seconds). The recording will automatically stop when the limit is reached. This is sufficient for quick microphone tests and audio checks.
Q: Can I use this for professional audio testing?
While this tool provides good basic testing capabilities, it's best suited for quick checks before calls or basic troubleshooting rather than professional audio engineering.
Q: Are my recordings stored anywhere?
No! All recordings are processed locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy.
