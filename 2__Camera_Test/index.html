üí° What Does This Tool Do?

Camera Test is a powerful tool to test and verify your device's camera functionality. Test your webcam, front camera, or back camera, capture high-quality photos, and download them instantly. Perfect for checking camera quality before important video calls or verifying camera hardware.

üöÄ Key Features

  • Live Camera Preview: See your camera feed in real-time with high quality
  • Switch Cameras: Easily toggle between front and back cameras
  • Capture Photos: Take high-resolution photos with a single click
  • Download Images: Save captured photos instantly to your device
  • Camera Info: View resolution, camera type, and photo count
  • Dark & Light Themes: Choose your preferred viewing mode

üìù How to Use

  • Step 1: Click "Start Camera" to activate your camera (grant permission if prompted)
  • Step 2: View your live camera feed and check the resolution
  • Step 3: Use "Switch Camera" to toggle between front and back cameras
  • Step 4: Click "Capture Photo" to take a picture
  • Step 5: Preview your photo and click "Download" to save it

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is the camera not starting?
Make sure you've granted camera permissions when prompted by your browser. Check your browser settings if the camera still doesn't work.
Q: Can I use this on mobile devices?
Yes! This tool works perfectly on mobile devices and automatically detects available cameras (front/back).
Q: What resolution photos can I capture?
The tool captures photos at your camera's maximum resolution (typically 1920x1080 or higher, depending on your device).
Q: Are my photos stored anywhere?
No! All photos are processed locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Your privacy is protected.
