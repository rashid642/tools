üì∑ About QR Code Scanner

What is this tool?

QR Code Scanner is a free online tool that allows you to decode QR codes and barcodes from images. Simply upload an image containing a QR code, and we'll extract the embedded data - whether it's a URL, text, contact information, WiFi credentials, or any other encoded data.

How to use:

  • Upload Image: Drag & drop an image or click "Choose Image" to select a file from your device
  • Preview: Review the uploaded image to ensure the QR code is visible and clear
  • Scan: Click "Scan QR Code" to decode the image
  • View Results: See the decoded data, which could be a URL, text, or other information
  • Take Action: Copy the data, or if it's a URL, open it directly in a new tab

Supported formats:

  • QR Code (most common)
  • Data Matrix
  • EAN-8, EAN-13
  • UPC-A, UPC-E
  • Code 39, Code 128
  • And many more barcode formats

Privacy & Security:

Your privacy is important. Images are processed on our servers but are not stored permanently. The scanned data is only visible to you and is not logged or saved.

FAQs:

Q: What image formats are supported?
A: We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, and WebP.

Q: What if my QR code isn't being detected?
A: Ensure the image is clear, well-lit, and the QR code is not damaged or obscured. Try taking a new photo with better lighting.

Q: Can I scan multiple QR codes at once?
A: Yes! If your image contains multiple QR codes or barcodes, we'll detect and decode all of them.

Q: Is there a file size limit?
A: For best performance, we recommend images under 10MB.
