üí° What Does This Tool Do?

Multi-Search Highlighter allows you to search for up to 5 different words or phrases simultaneously in your text. Each search term is highlighted with a different color, and you can easily navigate through all occurrences. Perfect for analyzing documents, finding patterns, or reviewing large texts.

üöÄ Key Features

  • 5 Simultaneous Searches: Find up to 5 different terms at once
  • Color-Coded Highlights: Each term gets its own distinct color
  • Occurrence Counter: See how many times each term appears
  • Easy Navigation: Move through matches with Previous/Next buttons
  • Auto-Scroll: Automatically scrolls to the current match
  • Case Sensitivity: Toggle case-sensitive search on/off
  • Whole Word Match: Search for complete words only
  • Large Text Support: Handle up to 50,000 characters

üìù How to Use

  • Step 1: Paste or type your text in the text area
  • Step 2: Enter search terms in the search boxes (up to 5)
  • Step 3: Watch as terms are highlighted with different colors
  • Step 4: Use Prev/Next buttons to navigate through matches
  • Step 5: Toggle case sensitivity or whole word options if needed

‚ùì FAQs

Q: What's the maximum text length?
You can search through up to 50,000 characters. This is roughly equivalent to 8,000-10,000 words.
Q: Can I search for phrases?
Yes! You can search for single words or multi-word phrases. Just type the complete phrase in any search box.
Q: What does "Whole Words Only" do?
When enabled, this option only matches complete words. For example, searching "cat" won't match "category" or "scatter".
Q: Is my text stored or sent anywhere?
No! All searching and highlighting happens directly in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.
