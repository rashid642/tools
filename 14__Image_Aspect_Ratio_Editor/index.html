‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What aspect ratios are available?
We offer 16:9, 9:16, 4:3, 3:2, 1:1 (square), 2:3, and custom ratios. These cover most social media and standard use cases.
Q: Is my image uploaded to a server?
No! All image processing happens entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.
Q: What image formats are supported?
You can upload JPG, PNG, WebP, and most common image formats. The output will always be PNG for best quality.
Q: Does cropping reduce image quality?
The cropped area maintains the original image quality. We don't compress or reduce quality during the crop process.
