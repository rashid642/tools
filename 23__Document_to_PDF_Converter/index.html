About Document to PDF Converter

Convert your Microsoft Office documents to PDF format quickly and easily.

Features

  • Convert Word documents (DOC, DOCX) to PDF
  • Convert PowerPoint presentations (PPT, PPTX) to PDF
  • Convert Excel spreadsheets (XLS, XLSX) to PDF
  • Preserves original formatting and layout
  • Fast server-side conversion using LibreOffice

How to Use

  • Click the upload area or drag and drop your document
  • Click "Convert to PDF"
  • Wait for the conversion to complete
  • Download your converted PDF file

Privacy & Security

All files are processed securely on our server and automatically deleted after conversion. We never store or share your documents.
