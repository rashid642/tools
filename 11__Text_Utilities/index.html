üìù About Text Utilities

üí° What Does This Tool Do?

Text Utilities is a comprehensive text processing tool designed for writers, developers, and content creators. Count characters and words, transform text case, find and replace text, and analyze your content with detailed statistics. All processing is done locally in your browser - your text never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

üöÄ Key Features

  • Real-time Statistics: Character count (with/without spaces), word count, line count, sentence count, paragraph count, and reading time
  • Case Transformations: Convert to UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, or Sentence case
  • Text Manipulation: Reverse text, remove extra spaces, remove line breaks
  • Find & Replace: Search and replace text with case-sensitive/insensitive options
  • Find & Highlight: Visually highlight all occurrences of search terms
  • Copy to Clipboard: Quick copy functionality for processed text
  • Dark Mode: Toggle between light and dark themes
  • 100% Privacy: All processing happens locally in your browser

üìù How to Use

  • Step 1: Type or paste your text into the main text area
  • Step 2: View real-time statistics on the right panel (character count, word count, etc.)
  • Step 3: Use transformation buttons to convert case (uppercase, lowercase, title case, etc.)
  • Step 4: Use "Find & Replace" section to search and replace text
  • Step 5: Click "Find & Highlight" to visually highlight matches in yellow
  • Step 6: Use utility buttons to remove spaces, line breaks, or reverse text
  • Step 7: Copy your processed text or clear to start over

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my text data secure and private?
Absolutely! All text processing happens 100% locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server or stored anywhere. Your text remains completely private.
Q: What does "Characters (no spaces)" mean?
This counts all characters excluding any whitespace (spaces, tabs, and newlines). It's useful for content with strict character limits where spaces don't count.
Q: How is reading time calculated?
Reading time is calculated based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute, which is the standard for adult readers.
Q: Can I process large texts or documents?
Yes! The tool can handle large texts efficiently since everything runs locally in your browser. There's no file size limit.
Q: What's the difference between "Find & Replace" and "Find & Highlight"?
"Find & Replace" permanently replaces all occurrences of text. "Find & Highlight" visually highlights matches without changing your text, making it easy to review before making changes.
Q: Can I use this tool offline?
Once the page is loaded, all functionality works offline since no server communication is needed. You can even save the HTML file for offline use.
