üí° What Does This Tool Do?

Text Diff Comparison tool helps you find and visualize differences between two texts. Compare documents side-by-side with color-coded highlighting to easily spot additions, deletions, and modifications. Perfect for code review, document comparison, and tracking changes.

üöÄ Key Features

  • Side-by-Side View: Compare two texts simultaneously
  • Three Comparison Modes: Line-by-line, word-level, or character-level
  • Color-Coded Differences: Green for additions, red for deletions, yellow for modifications
  • Real-time Statistics: See counts of changes at a glance
  • Case Sensitivity: Toggle case-sensitive comparison
  • Whitespace Options: Ignore whitespace differences if needed
  • Unlimited Text: No character limits

üìù How to Use

  • Step 1: Paste your original text in the left panel
  • Step 2: Paste your modified text in the right panel
  • Step 3: Choose comparison mode (Line/Word/Character)
  • Step 4: Set options (case sensitivity, whitespace)
  • Step 5: Click "Compare Texts" to see differences

üé® Color Legend

‚ùì FAQs

Q: What's the difference between comparison modes?
Line-by-line: Compares entire lines (best for code/structured text). Word-level: Highlights individual word changes (best for prose). Character-level: Shows every character difference (most detailed).
Q: Is there a text length limit?
No! You can compare texts of any length. However, very large texts may take a moment to process.
Q: When should I use "Ignore Whitespace"?
Enable this when you want to focus on actual content changes and ignore formatting differences like spaces, tabs, or line breaks.
Q: Is my text stored anywhere?
No! All comparison happens in your browser. Your text never leaves your device and is not stored anywhere.
