photo_library About Image Arranger to PDF

lightbulb What Does This Tool Do?

Image Arranger to PDF is a powerful tool that lets you upload multiple images, arrange them in any order you want, and combine them into a single PDF document. Whether you're creating a photo album, compiling receipts, organizing scanned documents, or preparing a presentation, this tool gives you complete control over the final layout.

The intuitive drag-and-drop interface makes it incredibly easy to reorder your images. Simply drag an image to a new position, and the PDF will be generated with images in exactly that order. Each image becomes a separate page in your PDF, maintaining its original quality and aspect ratio.

help Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What image formats are supported?
The tool supports all common image formats including JPG/JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, and BMP. You can mix different formats in the same PDF.
Q: How do I change the order of images?
You have two easy methods: 1) Drag & Drop: Click and drag any image to a new position (works on both desktop and mobile with touch support). 2) Manual Entry: Click the blue position number on any image, type the new position you want (e.g., move image 5 to position 2), and click the checkmark. The images will automatically reorder and update their position numbers.
Q: Is there a limit on the number of images?
No, there is no hard limit on the number of images. However, processing a very large number of high-resolution images may take longer. For best performance, we recommend keeping it under 100 images per PDF.
Q: Will my images lose quality in the PDF?
No! Images are embedded in the PDF at their original resolution. We don't compress or reduce quality during the conversion process. Each image becomes a full page in the PDF, maintaining its aspect ratio.
Q: What happens to my uploaded images?
All images are processed on our server and automatically deleted immediately after the PDF is generated. We never store, access, or share your images. Your privacy is our top priority.
Q: Can I edit the PDF after generation?
The generated PDF contains your images as pages. If you need to make changes, you'll need to rearrange and regenerate the PDF using this tool. For PDF editing, check out our other PDF tools like PDF Merger or PDF Splitter.
Q: What page size is used for the PDF?
Each page automatically sizes to fit its image perfectly, preserving the original aspect ratio. This means landscape images create landscape pages, and portrait images create portrait pages.
Q: Does this work on mobile devices?
Yes! The tool is fully responsive and works great on mobile. You can upload images from your phone's camera or gallery. For reordering on mobile, you can use touch drag-and-drop (press and hold an image, then drag to move it) or simply tap the position number and enter the new position - perfect for precise ordering on smaller screens.
